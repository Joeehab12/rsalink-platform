RSA Executive Sponsorship Program Overview

The role of an RSA Executive Sponsor

  • The RSA Executive is requested to meet their executive peer at least twice per year via phone or in person.  The RSA Account Manager can help coordinate these meetings and structure the conversation around the needs of the customer.

The role of an RSA Account Manager

  • Inform, prepare, and enable your sponsor by keeping them updated on the account information such as account challenges, opportunities, etc.  Additionally, the Account Manager should establish a stable, ongoing relationship with the RSA sponsor and facilitate to coordinate meetings at least two times a year.

The anticipated program benefits incorporate three primary categories:

  • Customer
    • Demonstrate that we are committed and invested in their success
    • Receives guidance on RSA technology, trends, and solutions
  • Sponsor
    • Conducts strategic conversations to support growth and profitability
    • Ascertain opportunities, challenges, and gain customer perspective
  • Account
    • Executive support and awareness enables new perspectives and commitment
    • Accelerates and expands influence into new business
