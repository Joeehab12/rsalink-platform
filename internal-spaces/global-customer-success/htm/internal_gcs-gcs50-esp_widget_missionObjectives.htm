Global Services is excited to launch a new RSA Executive Sponsorship Program (ESP). The ESP pairs Senior RSA Executives with our valued customers and enables them to build long-term relationships to bring success.

The primary objective of the ESP program is the following:
  • Build and strengthen executive relationships
  • Enable trust as a solution provider
  • Foster relationships that go beyond business, such as common interests, charities, etc.
  • Increase our customer satisfaction and retention
