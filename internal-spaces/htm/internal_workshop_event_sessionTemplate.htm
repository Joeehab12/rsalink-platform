Summary

This RSA Link Workshop Training gives you tips and best practices for using the RSA Link platform to drive self-service customer solutions. The Customer Support manager leading the session will guide you through training content about our self-service strategy, why it matters and how the RSA Link platform empowers us to mobilize this strategy.

Workshop sessions include exercises on searching and navigating best practices to share with customers, how to properly link articles to support cases, engaging in RSA Link communities, producing professional, high-impact knowledge base articles and more.
